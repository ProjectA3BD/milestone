A 59-YEAR-OLD Australian teacher, holding dual New Zealand travel documents, has been arrested in north eastern Thailand on suspicion of child sex offences.

PETER Dundas Walbran, from Sydney, was detained on Wednesday at his apartment in the north eastern town of Ubon Rathchathani, 614 kilometres from Bangkok where he has been teaching at a local international school.
