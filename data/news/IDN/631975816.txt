Two women suspected of killing the half brother of North Korean dictator Kim Jong Un could face the death-penalty in Malaysia. They are currently in custody and will be formally charged in court on Wednesday. Photo: Getty Images.

KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia—Prosecutors will seek the death penalty for two women suspected of killing North Korean dictator Kim Jong Un’s half brother, authorities said, as a delegation from Pyongyang arrived here seeking the body of the dead man.
