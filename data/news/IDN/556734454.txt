JAKARTA, Indonesia—Indonesian authorities were investigating possible links to the militant group Islamic State after a suicide bomber on a motorcycle blew himself up in an attack on a police station in central Java early Tuesday.

Inspector General Condro Kirono, chief of the Central Java police, said one officer was injured when the bomber entered the grounds of police headquarters in the city of Solo. Police said they are still investigating the attacker’s identity, and that the situation is under control.
