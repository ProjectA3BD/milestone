KUALA LUMPUR, Nov 13 — The police detained a man last night in connection with a quarrel at the Low Yat Plaza here.

Dang Wangi police chief ACP Zainol Samah said the incident occurred about 7pm.

He said the incident involved a 27-year-old suspect who assaulted an employee at the complex.

It was learnt the suspect and the worker were earlier involved in an accident in Jalan Bulatan. ­— Bernama
