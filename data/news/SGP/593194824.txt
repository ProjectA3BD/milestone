Eight Arrested in Anti-Vice Blitz

The Police have arrested eight women, aged 25 to 47, during an operation that was conducted on 26 October 2016.

During the 13-hour operation, officers from Ang Mo Kio Division conducted raids in the Hougang and Kovan. Eight women were arrested for their suspected involvement in vice-related activities. Cash amounting to S$780, mobile phones, and related documents were also seized during the operation.

Preliminary investigations revealed that the suspects had advertised sexual services online and operated within the rented residential units. Investigations are ongoing.
