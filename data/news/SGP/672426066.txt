SINGAPORE—A Singapore court sentenced a former banker to 4½ years in prison after he pleaded guilty to a money-laundering offense and deception linked to his handling of allegedly misappropriated funds from a Malaysian state investment fund.

Yeo Jiawei, 34 years old, was central to the flow of funds linked to 1Malaysia Development Bhd., or 1MDB, in Singapore as a wealth manager at a branch of Swiss bank BSI SA, prosecutors alleged.
