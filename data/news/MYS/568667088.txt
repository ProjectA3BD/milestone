A 36-year-old medical specialist, attached to a dental clinic in Bangsar, was charged in a Sessions Court in Kuala Lumpur today with causing the death of Syed Alman Zain Syed Ali, the son-in-law of Deputy Prime Minister Ahmad Zahid Hamidi.

Dr Ting Teck Chin, who is an obstetrics and gynaecologist specialist, claimed trial to a charge of culpable homicide not amounting to murder under Section 304(b) of the Penal Code.
