PATTANI -- A man was shot dead while walking home from the local mosque in Thung Yang Daeng district on Monday night, police said.

Pol Capt Pornrat Kabsamut, a duty officer at Thung Yang Daeng police station, said attack occurred about 8pm at Buno Laeraw village in tambon Talo Maena.

Mahamakueseng Dueramae, 54, was returning from prayers when he was shot by a hidden killer.

He died on the spot.

Police were investigating.
