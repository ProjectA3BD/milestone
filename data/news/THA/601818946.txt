A benefit for Carrie Powell Rodgers will be held Sunday, November 4 from 1:00 to 6:00 p.m. at the Sequatchie County Rescue Squad. Rodgers, born and raised in the Sequatchie Valley and a 2001 graduate of Sequatchie County High School, was recently diagnosed with kidney failure and is awaiting surgery. There will be chili dogs, chips, drink and dessert served for $7 a plate. A silent auction is also planned, as well as baked goods for sale. For more information, contact Maggie Mitchell at (423) 800-3836 or Carolyn Powell at (423) 326-7953.TS43
