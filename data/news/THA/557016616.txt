Joy Luck, an Asian restaurant that's popular in the Highlands, recently opened its East End location.

You'll find the new location in 3,200 square feet of space at 9850 Von Allmen Court, which is off Brownsboro Road, near the Cabela's store in Old Brownsboro Crossings. We've been watching this project for a while and knew that the owners of Joy Luck had an interesting challenge with opening in that space.
