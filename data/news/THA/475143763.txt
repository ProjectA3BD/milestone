Bangladesh ambassador to Thailand Saida Muna Tasneem talks to Deputy Prime Minister and Defence Minister Prawit Wongsuwon at the Defence Ministry.

Thailand and Bangladesh have vowed to further strengthen defence and security cooperation.

Deputy Prime Minister and Defence Minister Prawit Wongsuwon and Bangladesh ambassador to Thailand Saida Muna Tasneem discussed the issue at the Defence Ministry on Saturday afternoon.

Ms Tasneem raised several issues and proposals regarding the expansion of defence cooperation between the two regional neighbours.

She proposed the creation of an alumni friendship forum between the Chulachomklao Royal Military Academy and Bangladesh Military Academy in which the two countries have regular officer training exchanges.

Both sides stressed the need for reviving regular exchanges of visits between defence leaderships of the two countries.

Ms Tasneem also emphasised the need to establish a security forum between the two countries to address the menace of transnational crimes including human trafficking, drug smuggling and terrorism.

Gen Prawit confirmed Thailand's strong resolve to work closely with Bangladesh, especially regarding proposals made by Ms Tasneem, and assured her Thailand is ready to cooperate with Bangladesh towards greater defence and security cooperation.
