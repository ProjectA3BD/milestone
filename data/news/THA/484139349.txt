TWO Thai men who hunted down and murdered a Melbourne man because he tried to stop their attack on another man have shown no remorse, prosecutors say.

SARUD Seehaverachart and Thatiya Terdputham fled to Thailand within hours of fatally stabbing Luke Mitchell outside a 7-Eleven in the early hours of May 24, 2009.
