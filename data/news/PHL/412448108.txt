ZAMBOANGA, Philippines: Fourteen members of militant group Abu Sayyaf were killed and 19 others injured in a firefight with the Philippine military in soutnern province of Sulu on Wednesday.

Two government soldiers were killed and another 16 wounded in the battle involving Philippine Army Scout Rangers and Special Forces and some 300 rebels, Xinhua news agency reported.

Rowena Muyuela, spokesperson of Western Mindanao Command, said the gun battle continued until 9 a.m. local time on Thursday in the mountainous areas of Patikul town.
