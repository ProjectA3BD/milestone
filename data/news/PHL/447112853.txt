MANILA, Philippines, July 8, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- Frost & Sullivan will be recognizing top companies in the ICT, logistics, energy, environment, building technologies and agriculture sectors at the 2015 Philippines Excellence Awards at the Shangri-la Makati on July 16.

Mr. Ajay Sunder, Vice President at Frost & Sullivan Asia Pacific said that the Philippines Awards program first started in 2012, showcasing outstanding IT and telecommunications companies in the local market.

This year, a total of 26 awards will be presented to various companies in the ICT, logistics, building technologies, energy, environment and agriculture sectors.

"We believe that a comprehensive analysis on best practices is an invaluable source to companies. Such an analysis will also enable the companies to formulate strategies and to better their business processes which will ultimately drive corporate growth," Mr. Sunder added.

The 2015 Philippines Excellence Awards will recognize companies demonstrating best practices across various industries in the Philippine market. The objective of this awards program is to spur these industries forward by highlighting leading companies and individuals who inspire and influence the industry's performance.

The 2015 Philippines Excellence Awards recipients are identified based on in-depth interviews, analysis, and extensive secondary research conducted by Frost & Sullivan's analysts. Companies are typically studied on their revenue growth, market share in a specific category and growth in market share, leadership in new product introduction and innovation, breadth of products and solutions, major customer acquisitions, as well as business and market strategy in order to identify best practices.

For more information on the awards, please visit http://www.frost-apac.com/philippinesawards/
