MANILA, Philippines - President Aquino lauded members of the Association of Philippine Physicians in America (APPA) for their contribution to the country even if they are based in the US.

In his speech at the group's joint congress with the Philippine Medical Association, Aquino thanked the medical professionals for sending medical aid in the wake of calamities in the country such as the Mount Pinatubo eruption in 1991 and super typhoon Yolanda in 2013.

Aquino also took pride in his administration's efforts to improve health care, pointing out the substantial increase in the budget of the Department of Health and programs to provide health services for the poor.

He also underscored the need for the private sector to play an active role in improving the quality of health care in the country.

Also present in the event was Acting Health Secretary Janette Garin.
